## üìù Notes for Generation

- **Max's Consistency:** Always use references from pomerania-police.jpg or pomerania-pajamas.jpg depending on context
- **Scale:** Max should appear 1/10 the size of buildings (station)
- **Lighting:** Keep white fur bright in all scenes
- **Color Transitions:** Smoothly transition from warm tones (act 1) to institutional tones (act 3) and back to warm (act 4)
