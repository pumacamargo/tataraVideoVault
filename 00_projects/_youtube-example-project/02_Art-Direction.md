## üé¨ Visual Style

**General Tone:** Humorous but epic
- Professional cinematography of a small great hero
- Comic moments dramatically underlined
- Scale contrast: small Max vs big world

**Visual Reference:**
- Movies: Zootopia (cute but epic), Despicable Me (adorable but dramatic)
- Animation: Pixar, Disney (quality cinematography)
- Tone: Family + Comedy + Light Drama

**Composition:**
- Rule of thirds for epic moments
- Close-ups for adorable expressions
- Wide shots to contrast scale
- Depth with bokeh in backgrounds

**Lighting:**
- Day scenes: Natural, warm light
- Action scenes: Dramatic lighting
- Interiors: 3-point (warm key light, soft fill)
- Highlights on white fur (bokeh)
